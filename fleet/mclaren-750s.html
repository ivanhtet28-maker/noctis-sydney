Vehicle description

The 750S Spider, briefly.

The lightest car in its class — 1,326 kg dry — and the loudest argument for the convertible supercar still being a category. The 4.0-litre twin-turbo V8 delivers 740 horsepower and 800 Nm through a 7-speed dual-clutch. Roof down in 11 seconds at speeds up to 50 km/h. 0–100 km/h in 2.7 seconds, 332 km/h top speed, carbon-fibre monocell, hydraulic steering rack, no rear screen — only what the pace demands.
