The questions
before the keys.

Insurance & excess +

Every car is insured comprehensively for the duration of your reservation. Standard excess sits between A$5,000 and A$15,000 depending on vehicle. Excess waiver is available on request and is included on weekly bookings of the Spectre and Purosangue.

Driver requirements +

Full Australian or international licence, held for at least three years, minimum age 30 (35 for the McLaren and Ferrari). We verify identity discreetly via a single short video call. No counter, no queue, no paperwork left behind.

Mileage & range +

300 km included per day, 1,500 km per week — generous enough for a Bowral weekend or a Palm Beach loop. Beyond that, A$3.50/km. Cars may be driven anywhere in NSW; interstate by arrangement.

Delivery & collection +

Complimentary across Greater Sydney, Northern Beaches, and the Southern Highlands. Hunter Valley and Bowral on a small flat fee. Beyond that, by quote. Available 24 hours, including jetty and helipad handovers.

Cancellation +

Full refund up to 48 hours before delivery. Inside 48 hours, the deposit is held against a future booking — never lost. If we miss our delivery window by more than fifteen minutes, the day is on us.

Privacy +

Your details are read by a single member of our concierge team and held only for the duration of your stay. We are not on social media as a company. We do not photograph or post about our guests. Discretion isn't a feature — it's the house.
